Metal Roofing & Roof Replacement

TBRC Roofing has extensive repair, replacement, and restoration experience in not only metal, but all types of roof systems. When a roof has reached the end of its life, we install systems engineered for the Texas climate and backed by leading warranties.

Single-Ply Retrofit Roofing System

A single-ply metal retrofit roof involves custom cutting insulation, usually 1½″ to 3″ thick, to fit between the ribs in your roof to create a smooth, flat surface. Next, TBRC installs an additional 1″ of insulation (ISO) on top, then an energy-efficient white single-ply membrane.

These roofing systems are so effective at resisting extreme temperature and weather that they've proven to reduce a building's energy cost by as much as one-third.

  • Up to a 20-year NDL warranty
  • 2-inch hail impact resistance
  • Bonded and insured installation
  • Energy cost reduction up to one-third

Flat & Low-Slope Systems

Many commercial and industrial facilities are built with flat or very low-sloped roofs, where ponding water and freeze/thaw cycles cause leaks. TBRC solves this with positive drainage using tapered insulation (EPS) covered by a watertight, energy-efficient single-ply membrane. All of our single-ply retrofit systems can come with full, No Dollar Limit warranty coverage for up to 20 years, including coverage for ponding water.
