Roof Restoration

Reinvigorate your existing roof — watertight, reflective, and built to last.

Elastomeric Roof Restoration

An elastomeric roof restoration is a fast, comprehensive, and very effective roofing solution. What makes TBRC's coatings so successful is the use of an industrial-grade caulking solution that ensures a watertight roof before the coating is ever applied.

Energy-Efficient Coating

All TBRC coated roofs include the pre-caulking of all seams, fasteners, and penetrations, followed by primer and two coats of a highly reflective, energy-efficient coating to preserve your roof and drastically decrease the temperature inside.

This approach reinvigorates your existing metal roof with no tear-off, no disruption to your business, and extraordinary energy-efficiency perks thanks to the strong temperature reflectivity of the elastomeric system.

  • Watertight before coating is applied
  • No tear-off, no business disruption
  • Highly reflective — cooler interior temperatures
  • Extends the life of your existing roof

Cool, White & Watertight™

Our Energy Star™ rated systems keep your business as much as 35% cooler and can save you as much as one-third on your energy costs, while protecting your roof against extreme temperatures and water damage.
