About TBRC Roofing

Leading the way in commercial and industrial roofing since 1994.

The best solutions for your roof problems.

If the roof to your business is leaking, call the commercial roofing experts at TBRC Roofing Company. Our expertise also includes extensive work in metal roof repair and restoration.

With over 25 years of experience, TBRC continues to specialize in industrial and commercial roof restoration, replacement, and repairs in the Southwest. We've installed or restored over 30,000,000 square feet of commercial roofs, using top-rated, energy-efficient systems with the best warranties available.
