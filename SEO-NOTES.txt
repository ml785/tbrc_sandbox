TBRC ROOFING — SEO SUMMARY
==========================

WHAT'S BEEN DONE (already in the site files)
--------------------------------------------
On every page:
  • Unique <title> and meta description
  • Canonical URL tag (prevents duplicate-content issues)
  • Open Graph tags (Facebook / LinkedIn / iMessage link previews)
  • Twitter Card tags (large-image previews on X)
  • Robots meta (index, follow)
  • Geo meta (Frisco, TX) for local relevance
  • Favicon + Apple touch icon (uses the logo)
  • Descriptive alt text on all images

Structured data (JSON-LD) — this is the big one for local search:
  • Homepage: full RoofingContractor / LocalBusiness schema with name,
    address, phone, hours, service area (TX + OK), founding year (1994),
    slogan, and a catalog of services. This is what lets Google show you
    as a real local business with rich details.
  • Service pages: Service schema (replacement, restoration, damage assessment)
  • All inner pages: BreadcrumbList schema (nicer search result display)

Site-wide files:
  • sitemap.xml  — lists all 14 pages for search engines
  • robots.txt   — allows crawling and points to the sitemap


WHAT YOU SHOULD DO AFTER LAUNCH (off-page — can't be done in code)
------------------------------------------------------------------
These matter as much as the on-page work for a roofing company:

1. GOOGLE BUSINESS PROFILE (highest priority)
   Claim/verify the listing at google.com/business for the Frisco address.
   This is the #1 driver of local roofing leads. Add photos (use the
   aerial project shots), services, hours, and service areas. Ask happy
   clients (Stacy Furniture, etc.) for Google reviews.

2. GOOGLE SEARCH CONSOLE
   Go to search.google.com/search-console, add the property, verify it,
   and submit:  https://tbrcroofing.com/sitemap.xml
   This tells Google to crawl all pages and shows you search performance.

3. BING WEBMASTER TOOLS
   Same idea at bing.com/webmasters — submit the sitemap. Quick win.

4. CONSISTENT NAP (Name / Address / Phone)
   Make sure the business name, "5899 Preston Road, Suite 101, Frisco,
   TX 75034", and phone numbers appear IDENTICALLY across the website,
   Google Business Profile, Facebook, BBB, and any directories. Google
   cross-checks these for local ranking.

5. UPDATE THE FACEBOOK LINK
   The schema references facebook.com/tbrcroofingcompany. Confirm that's
   the right page, or send me the correct URL to update it.
