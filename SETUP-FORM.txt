TBRC ROOFING — CONTACT FORM SETUP (one step, ~2 minutes)
=========================================================

The quote form sends submissions straight to your inbox with one click —
no page reload, no email app opening. It uses Web3Forms (free, no account).

You just need to add your access key:

1. Go to  https://web3forms.com
2. Enter the email where you want leads to arrive (e.g. info@tbrcroofing.com)
3. They email you an "Access Key" — a long string of letters/numbers.
4. Open BOTH of these files in a text editor:
       contact.html
       free-inspection.html
5. In each, find this line near the top of the form:

       <input type="hidden" name="access_key" value="YOUR_ACCESS_KEY_HERE">

   Replace  YOUR_ACCESS_KEY_HERE  with the key they emailed you.
   (Keep the quotation marks.)

6. Save, and re-upload both files. Done — the form now sends on click.

That's it. No other changes needed.

----------------------------------------------------------------
NOTES
----------------------------------------------------------------
• Leads are emailed to whatever address you registered with Web3Forms.
  To change where they go, just update it in your Web3Forms settings.

• Until a real key is added, the form will tell visitors to call
  800-968-8272 instead (it does not expose any email address).

• A hidden anti-spam honeypot field is already built in.

• Web3Forms' free tier covers 250 submissions/month at no cost.
